I’m excited to share my latest personal project: a Self-Healing Platform built on Kubernetes! 🚀

As I continue to expand my skills in Cloud Architecture and DevOps, I wanted to build a system that doesn't just run, but actively maintains its own health and stability. 

I designed and deployed a cloud-native infrastructure on AWS EKS that automatically scales to handle traffic spikes and instantly recovers from unexpected failures. 

🛠️ Key Technologies Used:
• Amazon EKS (Kubernetes)
• Docker
• GitHub Actions
• Prometheus

💡 Key Outcomes:
• Automated the full deployment lifecycle with a robust CI/CD pipeline.
• Implemented self-healing mechanics (Liveness/Readiness probes) to ensure high availability and zero-downtime recovery.
• Configured Auto-Scaling (HPA) to dynamically allocate resources based on real-time CPU metric tracking.
• Gained hands-on experience in building enterprise-grade resilience.
